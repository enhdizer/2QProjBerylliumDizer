a palette is defined as "the range of colors used by a particular artist or in a particular picture."

when making a palette for your pixelart, it is important to not only manipulate the value or darkness of your colors, but also the saturation and hue. making use of those 3 values allows for a more pleasant-looking palette and more control over contrast.

MortMort has an excellent in-depth video on how to pick good colors for your palettes.

video from youtube.com/c/mortmort

when making a piece, start by blocking out the shape you want with colors in your palette, not by using lineart to create the shape.

as you shade the sprite, make adjustments to the shape and colors as needed.

most importantly, don't get demotivated if you can't seem to be satisfied with your work. everyone starts somewhere, and it is important to remember that the more you practice, the more you improve.
